Case Study

Digital Travel Itinerary Planner - HKGo!

Traveling around has always been a significant activity for people to relax themselves and explore the world. During the COVID-19 pandemic, there were lockdowns in different regions including Hong Kong, hence increasing the demand of local tourism. Up till now, the long-silent tourism industry has come back to life and there is a wave of revenge traveling with travelers looking for diversity in their travel experience.

HKGo! is a mobile application which aims at guiding travellers in planning their itineraries and broaden their social circles. At the same time promoting the beauty of Hong Kong and boost HK's tourism industry
