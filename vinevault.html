What is VineVault?

A platform for wine lovers to explore their wine journeys easily. Consumers can make online purchases, make bulk purchases, make comparisons among similar wines, choose their best-fit alcoholic beverages and keep records of their perferences.

Problem Statement

Hong Kong people are often busy and lack time to make purchases from physical stores. Wine beginners are often frustrated with the wide variety of choices and have difficulty finding their preferences.
