Project Overview

Project Objective

The project objective is to design a convenient and engaging mobile application which enhances the travel experiences for local and global travelers, allowing them to plan itineraries, know new friends, provide and gain insights.

Primary Audience

Tech-savvy users ages 18-50 who lack travel experiences or itinerary organizational skills and are looking for a tool to arrange their itineraries and gain insights.

Secondary Audience

Experienced travelers and small local businesses to make activities recommendations or promote their businesses.

Problem Statement

Overabundant sponsored contents

Limits users’ choice of destinations and leads to business problems such as low customer retention rate.

Lack of Communication

New users may feel lonely and lost as they may look for people with same interest and look for sense of belonging. Also leading to business problems including reduce of opportunity in market expansion and low retention rate.

Lack of Personalization

Users may feel frustrated when they have less control and may lower the efficiency in itinerary planning. It may lead to business problems such as high turnover rate and loss of uniqueness.

Accessibility Issues

users cannot plan their itineraries whenever and wherever they want. It also leads to business problems including high conversion rates and low retention rates.

The Design Process

Web --> Mobile Application

Most of the itinerary online were Web Applications, which are inconvenient to use.

To solve the accessibility issue and achieve convenience. Mobile application is designed to facilitate users' planning regardless of time and location.

The prototype beside introduce the features of HKGo! and allows users to perform log-in and sign-up actions.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Providing Templates

To solve the problem of inconvenience and unorganized itineraries, templates are provided to users such that they can focus on the content of the itinerary rather than the layout of their schedules, thus enhancing the usability and retention.

Templates are provided while users are planning. Users can make fast planning within few clicks of action, minimizing the time required to perform planning action.

While creating an itinerary, users are required to fill in information including name, duration, participants and choose their itinerary types and categories. The application will then create a weekly calendar view where they can insert activities day-by-day.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Personalization

To solve the issue of inadequate personalization, several elements including colored tags, photo uploads, and recommendations are designed to enhance users' experience, retention and loyalty.

Personalization - Colored Tags

While users are planning their itineraries, colored tags can be added for activities classifications and users can add more tags based on their personal needs. This facilitates users' planning process as they can make efficient categorization.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Personalization - Photo uploads and Ratings

Another personalization features designed are photo uploads and rating function. These features keeps record of users journey to different places where they could make ratings and comments to their itineraries at the same time attaching photos to the itineraries such that when they look back to their itineraries, they could get insights as well as recalling their happy memories during their trip.

Photo uploads and rating function not only raise users' experience but also increase their retention and loyalty to the application, making them long term users in the future as they develop their profile.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Personalization - Recommendation System

As users were signing up for an account, they were asked to choose their preferences. The system will display itineraries according to users' category preferences. In such way users can easily gain insights rather than browse through the entire application to find itineraries that may match their needs.

In such way, it reduces users' searching time, enhance users' experience and increase their retention as they can efficiently find matching itineraries.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Expansion of Community - Forum

Another key feature within the application is Forum. Users can create blog post with texts and media to share their experiences and opinions. It act as a community for ideas sharing and exchange.

Users can also comment, like or share the blog posts to show their support or appreciation to the author.

With forum, it can increase users' attachment to the application as they can gain new insights and plan their itineraries effectively.

Another key feature is the use of Hashtags(#). Hashtags allows users to effectively find relevant information within the large amount of blog post. It can also act as a tool for users to conduct campaign in the application such that users' sense of belonging will be enhanced.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Expansion of Community - Chat Rooms

To further expand the community, Chat room is designed for users to make new friends and facilitates the exchange of ideas.

Users can make direct messages to other users and exchange their ideas and even know travel buddies. Other that that, chat groups can be created such that people with the same interests can share their ideas in the group. In such way, users' sense of belonging will be enhanced and their retention will increase.

The Design Process (Cont.)

Activities Proposing

To solve the problem of overabundant sponsored contents, activities proposal feature is designed. Users can propose any interesting activities that are not within the system. This offers more choices to users at the same time allowing small businesses such as small workshops or local bizaar to promote themselves.

In terms of business goals, the feature allows higher user retention rates and expansion of market as they could find interesting activities from it and more business will be willing to participate in, boosting HK's tourism by adding diversities to it.

Moreover, activity proposing can ensure the sustainability of the application as the activities are always changing. It ensures the activities and itineraries within the application are always up-to-date.

Results

This section illustrates the success metrics that will be used to gauge and measure the impact of the application on user problems and business problems.

User Satisfaction Score

User satisfaction can be measured through surveys and feedback forms. The metric can be used to determine whether the accessibility issues can be addressed and make further improvements

Usability Test Results

Impact of HKGo! can be evaluate through tasks completion rates, time spent on tasks and error rates. It can help assess the effectiveness of each functionalities.

User Retention Rate

It can be measured by assessing users' revisiting to HKGo! over a period of time. High retention rate indicates the app features are valuable and easy to use, also helps determine whether to keep the functionalities.

Feature Usage Rate

It can be gauged and determine which feature(s) is the most popular, can gain insight on how the feature(s) cater users' needs and thus develop relevant features in the future.

User Engagement Rate

Assess the time users spent on the application and determine whether features such as forums and chat rooms can increase their single visiting time and loyalty towards the application.
